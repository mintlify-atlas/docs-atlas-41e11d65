## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register with your name, email, and a profile photo. Mydva uses JWT-secured sessions to keep your health data private.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the dosha quiz">
    Answer questions about your physical traits, diet, sleep, and temperament. The platform calculates your Vata, Pitta, and Kapha percentages and identifies your primary constitution.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your consultation">
    Fill in your personal health profile — age, weight, medical history, lifestyle, and health concerns. The AI consultation engine generates a structured plan covering diet, lifestyle, exercise, herbal remedies, and therapeutic treatments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore resources">
    Use the herbal remedy viewer, locate nearby Ayurvedic centers, and browse the education hub to complement your personalized guidance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technology stack

Mydva is built on a modern, three-tier architecture:

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|-----------|
| Frontend | React, Material UI, Redux |
| Dosha & AI backend | FastAPI (Python), Groq LLaMA 3.3 70B |
| Auth backend | Express.js, MongoDB, JWT |
| AI chatbot | Google Gemini 1.5 Flash (Flask) |

<Note>
  Mydva's consultation engine uses the Groq API to call LLaMA 3.3 70B. You need a valid Groq API key to enable AI-powered recommendations. See [Groq API configuration](/configuration/groq-api) for setup details.
</Note>
