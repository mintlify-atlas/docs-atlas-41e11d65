The personal consultation feature at `/dashboard/consultation` combines your dosha profile with detailed health and lifestyle information to generate a structured, individualized Ayurvedic wellness plan. The consultation is processed by an AI model trained in context-aware reasoning, which synthesizes your inputs against traditional Ayurvedic principles to produce practical, condition-specific guidance.

## How to complete the consultation form

The consultation form is divided into four sections. Complete all sections before submitting — the AI uses the full picture to produce relevant, coherent recommendations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Personal information">
    Provide your basic biometrics:

    - Age and gender
    - Weight and height

    Mydva automatically calculates your **BMI** from your weight and height. BMI is used alongside your dosha profile to contextualize dietary and lifestyle recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Medical history">
    Select any current or past conditions and list any medications you are currently taking.

    Recognized conditions and their Ayurvedic context are listed below. The AI builds condition-specific Ayurvedic framing into its prompt for each condition you select.

    <Accordion title="Recognized medical conditions">
      - **Diabetes** — Ayurvedic lens: Prameha; focus on Kapha and Pitta pacification, digestive fire (agni) regulation
      - **Hypertension** — Ayurvedic lens: Raktagata Vata; emphasis on Vata and Pitta reduction, stress management
      - **Arthritis** — Ayurvedic lens: Amavata or Sandhivata; focus on Vata pacification, ama (toxin) reduction
      - **Digestive issues** — Ayurvedic lens: Agni imbalance; tailored to the nature of the digestive disturbance (low, variable, or excess agni)
      - **Respiratory problems** — Ayurvedic lens: Pranavaha srotas; Kapha and Vata regulation, respiratory channel support
      - **Skin conditions** — Ayurvedic lens: Twak vikara; Pitta pacification, blood purification
      - **Sleep disorders** — Ayurvedic lens: Anidra; Vata and Pitta calming, nervous system nourishment
      - **Stress and anxiety** — Ayurvedic lens: Sahasa or Manasika roga; Vata stabilization, adaptogenic support
    </Accordion>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lifestyle">
    Describe your current daily habits:

    - **Diet type** — e.g., vegetarian, vegan, omnivore
    - **Physical activity level** — sedentary, light, moderate, or vigorous
    - **Sleep hours** — enter a value between 4 and 12 hours per night
    - **Stress level** — select low, medium, or high
  </Step>
  <Step title="Health concerns">
    Describe what you are hoping to address:

    - **Primary concerns** — free-text field for your main health goals or symptoms
    - **Previous treatments** — any Ayurvedic or conventional treatments you have tried before
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How the AI processes your data

After you submit the form, Mydva sends your complete health profile to the consultation engine, which uses the **Groq LLaMA 3.3 70B** model (`llama-3.3-70b-versatile`) at a conservative temperature of `0.3` to ensure focused, consistent output.

The model receives a structured prompt that includes:

- Your dosha profile (primary dosha, secondary dosha, percentages)
- Your biometrics and BMI
- Your selected medical conditions, each enriched with Ayurvedic context
- Your current medications
- Your lifestyle inputs
- Your stated health concerns and prior treatments

The low temperature setting (0.3) means the model produces more deterministic, clinically grounded responses rather than creative or exploratory ones — appropriate for health guidance.

## What you receive

Your consultation result is a structured document organized into the following sections:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Condition analysis" icon="stethoscope">
    An overview of how your reported conditions interact with your dosha constitution.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dosha impact analysis" icon="circle-nodes">
    How your current lifestyle and conditions are affecting your doshic balance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dietary recommendations" icon="utensils">
    Foods to favor and avoid, meal timing, and eating habits aligned with your dosha and conditions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lifestyle modifications" icon="sun">
    Daily routine (dinacharya) adjustments, sleep hygiene, and habit changes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Exercise recommendations" icon="person-running">
    Types of physical activity, intensity, and duration suited to your dosha and health status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Herbal remedies" icon="leaf">
    Specific herbs with dosage, timing, and preparation instructions tailored to your profile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Therapeutic treatments" icon="spa">
    Panchakarma or other Ayurvedic therapeutic approaches relevant to your conditions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Warnings and precautions" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Interactions to be aware of, contraindications with medications, and when to seek in-person care.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
The personal consultation is designed to complement — not replace — the guidance of a qualified Ayurvedic practitioner or licensed medical professional. Mydva's consultation output is informational and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always discuss significant health changes with your doctor, especially if you are managing a chronic condition or taking prescription medications.
</Warning>

## Saving and revisiting your consultation

Your consultation results are saved to your account. You can return to `/dashboard/consultation` to review past results or submit a new consultation if your health situation changes.

<Tip>
Update your dosha analysis before submitting a new consultation if significant time has passed or your lifestyle has changed. This ensures the AI has the most accurate constitutional context.
</Tip>
