In Ayurveda, every person is born with a unique combination of three biological energies called **doshas**—Vata, Pitta, and Kapha. This combination, known as your *prakriti*, shapes your physical traits, mental tendencies, digestive patterns, and susceptibility to imbalance. Understanding your dosha profile is the foundation of Ayurvedic wellness and the starting point for all personalized guidance in Mydva.

## The three doshas

The doshas are derived from the five elements of nature and govern different physiological and psychological functions in the body.

| Dosha | Elements | Primary traits | Governs |
|-------|----------|----------------|---------|
| **Vata** | Air + Ether | Dry, light, cold, mobile, subtle | Movement, circulation, breathing, nerve impulses |
| **Pitta** | Fire + Water | Hot, sharp, light, oily, liquid | Digestion, metabolism, body temperature, intellect |
| **Kapha** | Earth + Water | Heavy, slow, cool, oily, smooth | Structure, lubrication, immunity, emotional stability |

Most people have one dominant dosha (primary) and a secondary dosha that also influences their constitution.

## How the dosha quiz works

The quiz at `/dashboard/dosha-analysis` presents a series of questions about your body, mind, and habits. Each question maps to a physical trait or behavioral tendency and captures how closely it matches Vata, Pitta, or Kapha characteristics.

Topics covered in the quiz include:

- **Body traits** — frame, weight, skin texture, hair type
- **Temperament** — how you respond to stress, decision-making style, emotional patterns
- **Diet preferences** — appetite strength, food cravings, digestion speed
- **Sleep patterns** — duration, quality, dreams

For each answer you provide, the system records a `vata_score`, `pitta_score`, and `kapha_score` for that characteristic. These scores accumulate across all questions.

## How scores are calculated

After you complete all questions, the `DoshaAnalyzer` service totals your scores across all three doshas and converts them into percentages.

```python
# Simplified from backend/app/services/dosha_analyzer.py
total = total_vata + total_pitta + total_kapha

vata_percentage = (total_vata / total) * 100
pitta_percentage = (total_pitta / total) * 100
kapha_percentage = (total_kapha / total) * 100
```

**Primary dosha** is whichever percentage is highest.

**Secondary dosha** is assigned when any of the other two doshas scores above 25%. This reflects the common Ayurvedic understanding that most people are *dual-doshic* — predominantly one dosha with meaningful influence from a second.
