After signing in, the Mydva dashboard is your central hub for all wellness features. Every tool the platform offers — from your dosha quiz to AI consultations and herbal remedy exploration — is accessible from here. Your dashboard is private and requires an active session; any attempt to reach it without signing in redirects you to the login page.

## Dashboard layout

The dashboard is built around three main areas:

- **Navbar** — runs across the top of the screen. On desktop it displays your profile and navigation controls. On mobile, it includes a hamburger menu icon that opens the sidebar as a slide-in drawer.
- **Sidebar** — the primary navigation panel listing all dashboard sections. On desktop it is always visible. On mobile, toggle it open and closed using the hamburger icon in the Navbar.
- **Main content area** — the central panel that renders the selected feature. This area updates as you navigate between sections.

## Navigating the sidebar

The sidebar lists every section of your wellness dashboard. Click any item to load that feature in the main content area.

| Sidebar item | What it does |
|---|---|
| Home | Returns you to the dashboard overview with quick-access cards to all features |
| Dosha Analysis | Opens the interactive dosha quiz and displays your saved constitution results |
| Consultation | Takes you to the AI-powered health consultation form |
| Herbal Remedy | Launches the 3D herbal remedy browser |
| Centers | Shows a map and list of nearby Ayurvedic hospitals and clinics |
| Education | Opens the education hub with articles and videos on Ayurveda |

<Tip>
  On mobile, tap the hamburger icon in the top-left corner of the Navbar to open the sidebar. Tap it again or tap outside the drawer to close it.
</Tip>

## Your personalized data

Your dosha analysis results are stored in your account after you complete the quiz. These results carry forward into the consultation tool, which uses your constitution to shape every recommendation it generates. You do not need to re-enter your dosha manually — the consultation form reads from your saved profile automatically.

<Info>
  If you retake the dosha quiz, your results are updated. This also updates the baseline used by the consultation engine on your next session.
</Info>

## Getting the most out of Mydva

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the dosha analysis first">
    Your constitution results feed into every other feature. Take the quiz before using the consultation tool for the most personalized experience.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your health profile for consultations">
    The more detail you provide — age, weight, medical history, lifestyle habits, and current health concerns — the more specific and actionable your AI-generated plan will be.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the education hub to understand your results">
    After receiving your dosha breakdown and consultation plan, browse the education hub to learn what your constitution means and how Ayurvedic principles apply to your daily life.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore herbal remedies mentioned in your plan">
    Consultations often reference specific herbs. Use the herbal remedy viewer to explore 3D models and read traditional usage details for each one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect with a practitioner nearby">
    For in-person support, use the centers feature to find Ayurvedic hospitals and clinics in your area and book an appointment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your dashboard and all its data are tied to your account session. If your session expires after 3 days, sign in again to resume — all your saved results and history will be waiting. See [session management](/account/authentication) for details.
</Note>
