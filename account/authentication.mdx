Mydva uses secure, cookie-based sessions to keep you signed in between visits. Once you sign in with your email and password, your session lasts for 3 days before you need to authenticate again. This page explains how to sign in, what to expect during and after authentication, and how sessions behave over time.

## Signing in

Navigate to `/login` to access the sign-in page. Enter the email address and password you used when creating your account, then click **Login**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Open Mydva and navigate to `/login`. You are also redirected here automatically when trying to access any protected page without an active session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Type your registered email address and your password into the respective fields.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Login">
    Submit the form. If your credentials are correct, your session is established and you are redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after a successful login

When you sign in successfully, Mydva:

1. Establishes a secure session tied to your account
2. Redirects you to your dashboard at `/dashboard/`
3. Keeps you signed in for up to **3 days (72 hours)** without requiring you to enter your credentials again

As long as your session is active, you can close and reopen the browser and remain signed in.

## Session duration

Your session remains active for 3 days from the time you sign in. After 72 hours, your session expires automatically and you are returned to the login page on your next visit.

<Info>
  There is no "remember me" option — all sessions follow the same 3-day duration. To continue using Mydva after your session expires, simply sign in again.
</Info>

## Signing out

To end your session before the 3-day period is up, use the sign-out option available in the dashboard navigation. Signing out immediately invalidates your session and returns you to the login page.

## When your session expires

If your session has expired and you try to access any dashboard page, you are automatically redirected to `/login`. Your account and all saved data remain intact — sign in again to pick up where you left off.

## Error states

If your sign-in attempt does not succeed, Mydva displays one of the following messages:

| Error | What it means | What to do |
|---|---|---|
| All fields are required | One or both fields were left blank | Enter both your email and password before submitting |
| User not found | No account exists with that email address | Check for a typo or [create a new account](/account/registration) |
| Invalid password | The password does not match the account | Re-enter your password carefully; use your password manager if needed |

<Warning>
  If you have forgotten your password, contact Mydva support. Self-service password reset is not currently available in the platform.
</Warning>
