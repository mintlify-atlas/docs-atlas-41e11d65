Creating a Mydva account gives you access to the full suite of Ayurvedic wellness tools: dosha analysis, AI-powered health consultations, herbal remedy exploration, nearby center discovery, and the education hub. All of your quiz results and consultation history are tied to your account, so your personalized data is always available when you return.

<Note>
  A Mydva account is required to access the dashboard and all platform features. Public visitors can view the landing page but cannot access any wellness tools without signing in.
</Note>

## What you get with an account

Once registered and signed in, your account unlocks:

- **Dosha analysis** — take the interactive quiz and save your Vata, Pitta, and Kapha constitution results
- **AI consultations** — receive personalized dietary, lifestyle, and herbal recommendations based on your dosha profile
- **Herbal remedies** — explore 3D models of Ayurvedic herbs with usage guidance
- **Nearby centers** — find Ayurvedic hospitals and clinics close to your location
- **Education hub** — browse curated articles and videos on Ayurvedic principles

## Registration form

Navigate to `/register` to create your account. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Full name | Your display name as it appears across the platform |
| Email address | Used to identify your account and sign in |
| Password | Secures access to your account |
| Profile photo | A photo uploaded from your device — required to complete registration |

<Warning>
  All four fields are required. Registration will not proceed if any field is left blank or if no profile photo is selected.
</Warning>

## How to register

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the registration page">
    Open Mydva and navigate to `/register`, or click **Register** from the login page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your full name, email address, and a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a profile photo">
    Click the profile photo field and select an image file from your device. This photo appears on your dashboard and account profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Register**. If all fields are valid, your account is created immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    After successful registration, you are redirected to the login page. Enter your email and password to access your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After registration

Once your account is created, you will be taken to the `/login` page. Sign in with the email and password you just registered with to reach your dashboard and start your wellness journey.

<Tip>
  Complete your first dosha analysis right after signing in — your results flow directly into the consultation tool and personalize every recommendation you receive.
</Tip>

Already have an account? [Sign in to Mydva](/account/authentication).
